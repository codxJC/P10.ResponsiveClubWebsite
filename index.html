Activities

For our meetings we rent the lounge at the local library. We meet on Tuesdays and Thursdays 19:00 to 21:00 and on Sundays 17:00 to 19:00.

Weather permitted we organize outdoor fun workouts on Saturdays or social gatherings at local pubs and cafes.

Bootcamps

Our Bootcamps are intense and fun 45-60min workouts. The goal is to build strength and stamina. No single bootcamp is the same, we change things all the time to keep the workouts challenging and fun.

Flexibility & mobility

The bootcamp sessions are followed by 45-60min flexibility workouts.

Equipment

We provide most of the equipment, though it is recommended to bring your own exercise mat, a towel and a bottle of water or an energy drink to stay hydrated.
Yoga blocks are helpful for the flexibility section, but optional.
